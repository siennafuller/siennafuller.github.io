Georgina Fuller

London-based ceramics and glass artist

Georgina Fuller is a contemporary ceramics and glass artist working at the intersection of traditional craft techniques and modern sculptural practice. Her work explores the translucency and opacity of materials, investigating how light interacts with form to create immersive spatial experiences.

Glass
Ceramics
Collage

Her studio practice combines hand-building, wheel-throwing, and casting techniques with experimental glasswork. Each piece is carefully considered within the context of its intended space.
